## üõ† Implementation Steps

### For Each Product Page:

1. **Identify Category**
   - Is it an indoor plant? ‚Üí Use Category 1 keywords
   - Is it a garden tool? ‚Üí Use Category 4 keywords
   - etc.

2. **Select Focus Keyword**
   - Choose from "Primary Keywords" in that category
   - Use the primary keyword most relevant to the specific product
   - Example: For a money plant ‚Üí "indoor plants online" or "lucky plants"

3. **Configure Yoast SEO**
   - Paste Focus Keyword in Yoast "Focus Keyword" field
   - Paste Meta Description Template in Description field
   - Adjust template with specific product name if needed

4. **Write Product Description**
   - Aim for 300+ words
   - Include focus keyword naturally in first 100 words
   - Use related keywords from "Long-Tail Keywords" list
   - Follow content angle that fits the product
   - Add benefits specific to that product/category

5. **Set Page Title**
   - Format: `{Product Name} | Buy Online | Whole Lot of Nature`
   - Keep under 60 characters
   - Include primary keyword when possible
   - Example: "Money Plant Online | Buy Lucky Plant | Bangalore Delivery"

6. **Add Internal Links**
   - Link category pages from product pages
   - Link to related blog posts
   - Use anchor text with keywords
   - Example: "Explore more [air purifying plants](/shop/category/air-purifying)"
