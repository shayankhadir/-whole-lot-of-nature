## üöÄ How to Upload to WordPress

### Method 1: WordPress Admin (Recommended)
1. Go to: https://wholelotofnature.com/wp-admin/edit.php?post_type=product
2. Click on a product
3. Scroll to "Product image" section
4. Click "Set product image"
5. Upload your image
6. Click "Set product image" button

### Method 2: Bulk Upload
1. Go to: https://wholelotofnature.com/wp-admin/upload.php
2. Upload all images at once
3. Go back to products and assign images

## üí° Image Guidelines

### Optimal Specifications:
- **Size**: 800x800px minimum
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **File Size**: Under 500KB (compress if needed)
- **Background**: White or transparent
- **Orientation**: Square (1:1 ratio)

### Quick Image Compression:
- **TinyPNG** - https://tinypng.com/
- **Squoosh** - https://squoosh.app/
